Billing & Subscriptions

  • Subscriptions are managed by Apple. To cancel or change plans: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → ThryveFit.
  • Trials convert to paid unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the period ends.
  • Prices: Monthly $9.99 • Annual $79.99 (regional pricing may vary).

Troubleshooting

  • Restore purchases: In the app, open Premium → Restore Purchases.
  • Data not syncing: Ensure network access and restart the app.
  • Voice logging: Grant microphone permission in iOS Settings → ThryveFit.
  • Camera scanning: Grant camera permission in iOS Settings → ThryveFit.
