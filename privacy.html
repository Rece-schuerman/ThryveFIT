1. Information We Collect

  • Account & Contact: email and optional name.
  • App Data You Enter: meals, macros, workouts, notes, and related metadata.
  • Device & Usage: device model, OS/app version, diagnostics/crash logs, and basic analytics (e.g., screens viewed, feature usage).

2. How We Use Information

  • Operate core features (logging, plans, analytics).
  • Personalize content (macro targets, recommendations).
  • Maintain safety, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • Analyze aggregate usage and improve the app.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with service providers that help us run the app (e.g., analytics, crash reporting) under confidentiality obligations, or when required by law.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes above. You can request deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us at thryvefitllc@gmail.com. Deletions are processed within a reasonable timeframe unless we must retain data to comply with legal obligations.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data (subject to verification).
  • Opt‑out of non‑essential analytics where offered in‑app or by contacting us.

6. Children’s Privacy

ThryveFit is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. International Users

Your data may be processed in the United States or other locations where we or our providers operate and may be subject to local laws.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy with a new “Effective Date.” Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
